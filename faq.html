1. What is the standard cutoff for same-day delivery?

Orders must typically be placed by 1:00 PM in the recipient's local time zone.

2. Are flowers delivered in a cardboard box?

No. Orders are hand-delivered by local florists in water-secured arrangements.

3. Can I send flowers to a hospital or ICU?

Yes, but check hospital restrictions on fragrances and flowers before ordering.

4. What if the recipient is not home?

Couriers leave flowers in a safe location or with a neighbor when possible.
